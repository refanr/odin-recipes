Lasagne

Description

This is my lasagne dish

Ingredients

  • Minced meat
  • Milk
  • Lasagne package Knorr
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook minced meat in frying pan
  2. Add milk and water to frying pan
  3. Add sauce powder to frying pan
  4. Pour about 1/4th of the meat and sauce to an oven dish
  5. Arrange lasagne plates on top of the sauce in the oven dish
  6. Repeat the last to steps, ending with sauce on top
  7. Sprinkle on some shredded cheese
